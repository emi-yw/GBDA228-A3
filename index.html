Hello, welcome to

Tong Tong

In todays world so many people are struggling with their culture and self identity. My vision is to share my story and
experiences through illustrations of cartoon characters to create a safe space for other people to share their stories and to know
that they're not alone in how they feel. Hopefully by the end of summer 2023 this project will be up and launched with a
Instagram account and a few illustrations to be posted. I want this account to be available to all ages and all cultures, where
anyone can safely be themselves and learn about their own culture without being ashamed.
